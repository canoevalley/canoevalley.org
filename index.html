Canoe Valley School envisions a community where learning is a whole-person, lived experience, rooted in place, shaped by human and nonhuman relationships, and carried forward by the curiosity and agency of young people.

We imagine a school where self-directed, project-based learning invites students into meaningful encounters with the world around them, cultivating the confidence, creativity, and care needed to contribute to a more beautiful, just, and abundant future in an era where environmental and social challenges are proliferating.

In this vision, students grow not only in knowledge but in purpose, discovering who they are by engaging deeply with the work, people, and places that call them into becoming their most unique and fully realized selves.
